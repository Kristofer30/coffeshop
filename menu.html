Cappuccino

Flat White

an espresso drink consisting of a double shot of espresso and steamed milk with a thin layer of microfoam on top.

Espresso

a concentrated coffee beverage made by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee beans.

Matcha

a trendy drink made by combining freshly brewed filter coffee with matcha powder and milk or a plant-based alternative.

Salted Caramel

a popular flavor pairing that combines the sweetness of caramel with the savory taste of sea salt.

Macchiato

a coffee drink that translates to "stained coffee" in Italian, referring to the espresso being "stained" by a small amount of milk or milk foam.
